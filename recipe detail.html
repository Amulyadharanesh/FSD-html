Hyderabadi Biryani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, marinated
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • Saffron strands
  • Ghee
  • Salt
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Fry the sliced onions until golden brown and set aside.
  3. Cook the marinated chicken with tomatoes, yogurt, and spices until tender.
  4. Boil the rice until 70% cooked and drain.
  5. Layer the chicken and rice in a pot, adding fried onions, saffron milk, ghee, and fresh herbs.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with raita.
